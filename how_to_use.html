How to Use - Speech-to-Indian Sign Language Converter

Step-by-Step Instructions

  • Step 1: Access the Application

    Open the Speech-to-ISL Converter application on your browser or device to begin using it.

  • Step 2: Start Speech Input

    Click the microphone icon to begin speaking. Make sure to speak clearly for the best transcription results.

  • Step 3: View Real-Time Text Conversion

    Your speech will be converted into text in real-time, which will appear on the screen for you to review.

  • Step 4: Edit the Text (if necessary)

    If any part of the transcription is incorrect, you can manually edit the text before submitting it.

  • Step 5: Submit the Text

    Click the "Submit" button once you're satisfied with the transcription. The text will then be converted to Indian Sign Language gestures.

  • Step 6: View the ISL Gestures

    See the corresponding Indian Sign Language gestures displayed on the screen for visual understanding.

  • Step 7: Repeat the Process

    For another conversion, click the microphone again to start a new session and repeat the steps.

  • Step 8: Save or Share Your Results (optional)

    Depending on the app's functionality, you may save or share your results for easier communication with others.
